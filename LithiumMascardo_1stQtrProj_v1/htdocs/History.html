Fun History of Basketball

James Naismith created basketball in 1891 with the intention of making it a less dangerous sport than football. At the time he invented the winter-only sport that keeps players indoors, he was a PhD student, age 31. The game was immediately established, and it continued to gain popularity far into the 20th century.
